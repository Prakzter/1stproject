CNY (or LNY) spring cleaning for your website

It’s that time of the year… Chinese/ Lunar New Year! And as tradition goes, we usually do a big spring cleaning of the house. Ceiling fans are finally wiped, revealing the true colour of the fan blades underneath the grey dust coat. Windows get back their glean. Another thing that needs a big-time cleaning up of is… your website.

Over the months or years, random things creep into your website as you make changes here and there. It’s time to take a step back and take stock. It also makes great feng shui sense because, you know, things that aren’t taken care of just stagnants and doesn’t breathe fresh energy.

We break down the type of clutter your website might have accumulated (just like your old storeroom):

Outdated articles

Popular marketing books mention social media such as Facebook and Twitter. They no longer advocate distributing flyers or going door-to-door to improve your sales. Likewise, your articles have to keep up with the times. If they were written in 2012 and no longer relevant, it might be time to take them down.

Pages nobody visits

Your website is like the infrastructure of Singapore. If you have a random road that nobody uses, you could probably do away with it. The only reason it should be there is if you’re going to expand that area into a little town, add shops, industries, have someone use it. Or maybe it’s a little animal-crossing. It has to have a purpose.

Erase those stale pages and rethink how you want your map to be like.

Sorry, that page doesn’t exist

Broken links scream unkempt when it comes to your website. When the user clicks on a broken link and doesn’t get to where he/she thought it would go to, be sure that the next thing they plan on doing is to click that small x at the top left corner of your page.

Expired website designs

The trendy games in the past were playing with marbles and country flag erasers. Today, even Playstation is considered antique. Don’t let your website look like a marble trying to appeal to kids of today; it will never work! Your design has to look 2018 or it just isn’t going to turn any heads.



We hope that these pointers help in your website spring-cleaning! Keep your website clutter-free and minimalistic - covering just enough for your users to be happy.

From us at PageXsg, Happy Lunar New Year!
